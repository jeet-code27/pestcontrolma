Signs You May Have Bed Bugs

Many infestations go unnoticed during the early stages. Common warning signs include:

  • Small red bites on exposed skin
  • Blood spots on sheets or pillowcases
  • Dark fecal stains on mattresses
  • Shed bed bug skins
  • Live bed bugs around sleeping areas
  • Musty odors in heavily infested rooms

If you notice any of these signs, schedule an inspection as soon as possible.
